About Me

AI/ML Researcher & Developer

Hi! I'm Muhammad Ibrahim Zia, a passionate AI and Machine Learning researcher currently in my final year of high school. Despite my age, I've dedicated myself to understanding and advancing the field of artificial intelligence.

My journey into AI began with curiosity about how machines could learn and make decisions. What started as exploring basic algorithms has evolved into building complex neural networks, experimenting with state-of-the-art models, and contributing to the AI research community.

As an independent researcher, I focus on practical applications of machine learning, from computer vision to natural language processing. I believe in learning by doing, which is why I build projects from scratch to truly understand the underlying concepts.

When I'm not coding or training models, you can find me reading research papers, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my knowledge with the community.
